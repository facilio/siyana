# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality,
personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the accuracy of the knowledge base

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Knowingly submitting inaccurate data or copyrighted material

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the
maintainers by opening a confidential issue or contacting a repository maintainer.
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
