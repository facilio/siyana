A sectional glass-reinforced-plastic tank storing potable or domestic water, feeding booster sets and gravity systems. Standard cold-water storage in GCC buildings; a critical water-hygiene point given high temperatures.

## Region context

High ambient heat warms stored water and accelerates microbial growth, so tank insulation/shading, turnover, and the legally required periodic cleaning & disinfection are central to water safety.
